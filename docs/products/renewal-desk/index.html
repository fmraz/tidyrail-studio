Track renewals before they turn into surprises.

Renewal Desk is a free local-first tracker for subscriptions, warranties, service dates, and document expiry reminders. The current release stores data locally and does not require an account.

Problem and solution

Important dates are scattered. Renewal Desk gives them a home.

Subscription renewals, warranty endings, service dates, and document reminders often live in emails, receipts, notes, and memory. Renewal Desk keeps the next important date visible without turning the workflow into a complex platform.

Current free release includes

  • Add, edit, search, filter, and delete records
  • Dashboard stats and upcoming-renewal table
  • Receipt status and notes
  • JSON backup/import and CSV export
