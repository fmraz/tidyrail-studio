Cookie Policy.

Draft policy for a privacy-respecting product website.

Current use

The public website does not intentionally use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. The site may store small local preferences, such as a manually selected language.

Future account features

If cloud accounts are enabled, the authentication provider may use strictly necessary session storage or cookies to keep users signed in. This policy should be reviewed before public account launch.

Analytics

No invasive analytics are planned. If privacy-friendly analytics are added later, they must be disclosed before use.
