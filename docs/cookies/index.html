Cookie policy.

Tidyrail Studio avoids advertising cookies and third-party tracking in the current public release.

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Current use

The public website does not intentionally use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. The site may store small local preferences, such as a manually selected language.

Product storage

Renewal Desk stores product records in browser local storage on the device where the app is used. Users can export JSON backups and CSV files from the app.

Future account features

If cloud accounts are enabled, the authentication provider may use strictly necessary session storage or cookies to keep users signed in. This policy will be updated before those features are publicly enabled.

Analytics

No invasive analytics are included in the current release. If privacy-friendly analytics are added later, they must be disclosed before use.
