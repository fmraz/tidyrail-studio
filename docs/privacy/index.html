Privacy policy.

Tidyrail Studio is designed around minimal data collection, local-first products, and user-controlled exports.

Current products

Renewal Desk stores records locally in the browser in the current free version. Tidyrail Studio does not receive those local records unless a future cloud sync feature is explicitly enabled by the user.

Future accounts

Cloud sync is not active in the current public release. If accounts are enabled later, they should store only the information required for authentication, sync, export, deletion, and privacy settings.

Analytics

No invasive analytics or advertising tracking are planned. If privacy-friendly analytics are added later, this policy will be updated before use.

Exports and deletion

Users should be able to export their product data and delete local or cloud data when the relevant storage mode is active.

Draft notice

This policy is a draft and should be reviewed before a full public account launch.
