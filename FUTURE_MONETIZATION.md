# Future Monetization

Tidyrail Studio products are free for now. Do not implement payment processing, subscriptions, locked Pro features, artificial limits, or paid downloads yet.

## Current Strategy

- Build genuinely useful free tools.
- Make products easy to try without accounts.
- Earn trust through privacy-respecting design and clear documentation.
- Collect feedback before choosing monetization.
- Keep public claims honest and specific.

## Possible Future Options

### Optional Pro Versions

Add paid advanced features only if free users clearly need them. Do not remove useful existing free functionality to create artificial pressure.

### Paid Bundle

Offer a paid bundle after several tools are mature and free versions have proven demand.

### Donations

A donation link can be considered later, but only after the products are useful and public.

### Commercial License

A commercial license may make sense for small teams using tools in workplace workflows.

### Paid Templates

Some tools may support optional template packs, checklists, or workflow bundles.

### Premium Sync

Cloud sync could be paid later, but only if users ask for it and a privacy-respecting low-maintenance backend is approved.

### Sponsorship

Newsletter or website sponsorship could be explored later, but only if it does not compromise trust or add tracking-heavy advertising.

## Not Implemented Now

- No subscriptions.
- No payment processing.
- No Pro gates.
- No fake scarcity.
- No paid download pages.
- No account system.
- No tracking-based growth funnel.
