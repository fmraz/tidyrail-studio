## Who It Is For

Renewal Desk is for people who manage recurring personal, household, freelance, or office admin and want a simple place to remember what renews next.

## Problem

Renewal dates often live in inboxes, receipts, calendars, notes, and memory. That makes it easy to forget a warranty, miss a service date, or notice a subscription only after it renews.

## Benefits

- See upcoming renewals in one dashboard.
- Track which receipts are saved or missing.
- Keep notes close to each renewal record.
- Export backups whenever you want.
- Use it without an account in the MVP.

## Privacy Section

Renewal Desk stores MVP data locally in your browser on your device. It does not require an account, send tracker data to a server, or include analytics scripts.

## FAQ

**Does it cancel subscriptions?**

No. Renewal Desk is an organizer. It does not contact vendors or cancel anything for you.

**Can I back up my data?**

Yes. Export a JSON backup from the Export screen.

**Does it sync across devices?**

No. The MVP is local to the browser and device where it is used.

**Is it a financial tool?**

No. It is a date and record organizer. It does not provide financial advice.
