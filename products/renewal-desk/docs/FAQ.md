# Renewal Desk FAQ

## Who is Renewal Desk for?

Renewal Desk is for people who want a simple private place to track subscriptions, warranties, service renewals, and expiry dates.

## Does it require an account?

No. The MVP runs locally in the browser.

## Where is my data stored?

Records are stored in the browser's local storage on the device where the app is used.

## Can I back up my data?

Yes. Use Download backup to create a backup file.

## Can I import my data on another device?

Yes. Download a backup from one browser and restore it in Renewal Desk on another browser.

## Does Renewal Desk cancel subscriptions for me?

No. Renewal Desk is an organizer. It does not contact vendors, cancel subscriptions, or provide financial advice.
