# Renewal Desk Launch Copy

## Tagline

Track renewals before they turn into surprises.

## Short Description

Renewal Desk is a private local-first tracker for subscriptions, warranties, service dates, and document expiry reminders.

## Product Page Copy

Important dates often live in inboxes, receipts, calendars, and memory. Renewal Desk gives you one simple place to track what renews, what expires, what costs money, and which receipts still need to be found.

It runs locally in your browser, stores data on your device, and lets you export your records whenever you want.

## Feature List

- Upcoming renewal dashboard.
- Subscription, warranty, document, service, and custom categories.
- Receipt status tracking.
- Local search and filtering.
- JSON backup and import.
- CSV export.
- No account required in the MVP.
