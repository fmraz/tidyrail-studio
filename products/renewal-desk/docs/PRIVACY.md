# Renewal Desk Privacy Policy Draft

Effective date: Draft, not published.

Renewal Desk is designed as a local-first utility.

## Data Stored

The MVP stores renewal records in the browser's local storage on the device where the app is used. Records may include names, dates, categories, costs, receipt status, and notes entered by the user.

## Data Sent to Servers

The MVP does not send app data to Tidyrail Studio servers. It does not require an account and does not include analytics or tracking scripts.

## Exports

Users can export JSON backups and CSV files. Exported files are controlled by the user and may contain the information entered into the app.

## Limitations

Browser local storage can be cleared by the browser, the operating system, or the user. Users should export backups if they want to keep a copy of their records.
