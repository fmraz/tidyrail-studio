# Tidyrail Studio

Tidyrail Studio is an independent software studio building small, polished, privacy-respecting utilities for everyday administrative work.

## Positioning

Tidyrail Studio makes practical local-first tools that help people organize recurring life tasks, clean up messy information, and finish routine work faster without accounts, complex platforms, or unnecessary tracking.

## Product Philosophy

- Build small independent products, not a bundled ecosystem too early.
- Prefer local-first software that works without paid infrastructure.
- Solve problems that are understandable in one sentence.
- Ship useful MVPs quickly, then polish toward free public v1.0 quality.
- Avoid products that require medical, legal, financial, tax, gambling, adult, surveillance, malware, or deceptive functionality.
- Never claim outcomes that cannot be proven.
- Keep support burden low through clear UX, documentation, exports, and predictable behavior.

## Quality Standards

- Clear first-run experience and useful empty states.
- Local storage by default whenever practical.
- No unnecessary permissions, tracking, accounts, or paid APIs.
- Export and backup options for user-owned data.
- Honest product copy with no fake social proof.
- README, privacy policy draft, changelog, roadmap, support FAQ, and release checklist before any release.
- Manual QA of the core flow before release candidate status.

## Ethical Rules

- No fake reviews, testimonials, revenue, download counts, users, case studies, or scarcity.
- Account registration, domain purchase, public posting, marketplace submission, product publication, payments, and irreversible external actions require explicit approval for the concrete destination and action.
- No scraping private data or spammy community marketing.
- No misleading affiliation with platforms, celebrities, customers, or well-known brands.
- State uncertainty clearly where legal, trademark, market, or technical assumptions have not been verified.

## Final Name

The final working company name is **Tidyrail Studio**.

Reason: it is short, practical, memorable, not AI-hype-like, suitable for a portfolio of small organization utilities, and had no obvious high-profile collision in preliminary checks. Before buying a domain, publishing, or submitting products, run formal trademark and domain checks.

Current domain note: `tidyrail.com` resolves to an existing server. `tidyrail.studio`, `tidyrailstudio.com`, and `gettidyrail.com` did not resolve in a DNS spot check on 2026-06-25, but registrar availability must still be checked before purchase. VEDOS checkout later showed `tidyrailstudio.com` as the practical first purchase target because it was much cheaper than `tidyrail.studio`.
