# Sales Strategy

## Current Status

Sales are paused. Tidyrail Studio is currently focused on free products, public trust, feedback, and distribution.

Do not implement paid downloads, subscriptions, storefront checkout, locked Pro features, or artificial limitations.

## Portfolio Offer

- Individual tools: low-friction pricing between $9 and $29 depending on complexity.
- Bundle: 25-35% discount once at least three tools are stable.
- Commercial/small team license: optional for tools with workplace use.

## Product Page Structure

1. Product name and one-sentence value.
2. Who it is for.
3. Problem it solves.
4. Core workflow.
5. Privacy and local-first explanation.
6. Feature list.
7. Free download.
8. FAQ.
9. Known limitations.
10. Download or purchase button after approval.
