# Pricing Strategy

## Current Status

Pricing is paused. Tidyrail Studio products are free for now.

Do not add payment processing, subscriptions, locked Pro features, artificial limits, paid downloads, or checkout links.

Future monetization is documented separately in `FUTURE_MONETIZATION.md`.

## Previous Paid Ideas

The earlier price ranges below are historical planning notes only. They are not part of the current public launch.

| Product | Suggested Price | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| Renewal Desk | $12-19 one-time | Broad use, local-first, exportable data |
| CleanClip Local | $9-15 one-time | Lightweight, high-frequency utility |
| FolderForge | $12-19 one-time | Strong freelancer use case |
| CSV Tidy | $19-29 one-time | Higher workplace value, more QA burden |
| Packwise Lists | $7-12 one-time | Consumer-friendly, lower willingness to pay |

## Bundle Concept

Deferred until free products have real users, feedback, and stable distribution.
