# Marketing Strategy

## Positioning

Tidyrail Studio sells small utilities for people who want less admin friction without signing up for another platform.

## No-Budget Traffic Strategy

- Publish honest launch posts only where self-promotion is allowed.
- Use SEO pages for specific problems, not generic productivity hype.
- Offer useful free versions or demos where practical.
- Share product build notes transparently without fake traction claims.
- Prepare marketplace pages but do not publish without founder approval.

## Product Hunt Copy Template

**Tagline:** Local-first tools for everyday admin loose ends.

**Short description:** Tidyrail Studio builds small private utilities that help people track renewals, clean text, organize files, and finish routine work faster without accounts or unnecessary tracking.

## Reddit Post Rules

- Only post in communities that allow tool sharing or feedback requests.
- Lead with the problem and what was built.
- Disclose founder/building status.
- Do not claim popularity, revenue, users, or endorsements.
- Ask for feedback, not sales, unless the community explicitly allows launches.
