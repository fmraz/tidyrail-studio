# Account Setup Checklist

This checklist is for external setup. Product-facing text remains English. Founder coordination and progress reports are in Czech.

## Current Rule

Account registration, domain purchase, public posting, marketplace submission, and publication require explicit approval for the concrete destination and action.

Current explicit task: prepare VEDOS domain purchase through the founder's existing VEDOS account. Account email, credentials, and payment details are stored outside the public repository.

## Stop And Ask The Founder

- Payment details.
- Final purchase confirmation.
- Identity verification.
- Tax or payout information.
- CAPTCHA completion.
- 2FA/OTP codes.
- Public publication.
- Marketplace submission.
- Social posting.
- Email sending.

## Domain Account

Preferred domain: `tidyrailstudio.com`

Fallback domains:

- `tidyrail.studio`
- `gettidyrail.com`

Required founder handoff fields:

- Registrar account email.
- Password or password-manager action completed by founder.
- 2FA/OTP if required.
- Payment method entered by founder.
- Final confirmation by founder.

## Paid Storefronts

Paused. Do not register Lemon Squeezy, Gumroad, or paid storefront accounts for the current free-first launch unless explicitly re-approved.

## GitHub

Use only after deciding whether Renewal Desk should have a public free version or a private repository.

Recommended repository name if public:

- `renewal-desk`

Public repository requires concrete approval before creation.

## Product Hunt

Prepare account and launch draft only. Do not submit launch without concrete approval.
