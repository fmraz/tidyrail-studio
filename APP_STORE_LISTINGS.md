# Apple App Store and Mac App Store Listing Draft

Status: draft, do not submit without approval.

## App Name

Renewal Desk

## Subtitle

Track renewals and expiry dates

## Short Description

Renewal Desk helps you keep subscriptions, warranties, service dates, and expiry reminders in one clear place.

## Long Description

Renewal Desk is a focused organizer for recurring renewals and important dates. Track subscriptions, warranties, document expiries, and service reminders without turning everyday admin into a complex system.

The current product direction is privacy-first, export-friendly, and free while Tidyrail Studio collects feedback.
