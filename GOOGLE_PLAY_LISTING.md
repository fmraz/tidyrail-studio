## App Name

Renewal Desk

## Short Description

Track renewals, warranties, subscriptions, and expiry dates.

## Full Description

Renewal Desk is a simple productivity tool for keeping important recurring dates in one place. Use it to track subscriptions, warranty endings, service reminders, document expiries, and other dates that are easy to lose in email or notes.

The app is designed around clarity, privacy, and exportable data.
